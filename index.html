My Favorite Hobby:
Hiking

About

Hiking is my favorite hobby as it encourages me to get some fresh air, excercise, and explore the wilderness. In my preferance, I enjoy hiking in areas with many flowers, and some delightful terrain. I had developed my favor for hiking when I barely walked four miles on a trail in Hawaii. I had come to realize that, despite my aching body, I quite enjoyed myself. To sum up, I believe that hiking is an enjoyable hobby that anyone should consider trying.


My Favorite Places to Hike

As I see it, hiking under the warm sun, wild daisies all scattered amongst many rolling hills is my idea of a good hike. Sometimes, a shady path beneath a forest of trees becomes a thrilling adventure. Yet, going for a hike at noon will probably result in me sweating from the immense heat. That's why I suggest the perfect time for a hike to be during the weekend at about 6:30 to 7. One of my most preffered hiking spots is the Courdet Trail in Pleasanton, CA.
